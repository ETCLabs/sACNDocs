◆ active_sources

const sacn_remote_source_t* SacnRecvMergedData::active_sources

The handles of all sources considered to be active on the current universe.

Sources that are currently in a sampling period are not represented in the merged data and therefore aren't listed here either. This buffer is owned by the library.
